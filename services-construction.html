Construction

EMSI’s construction management professionals provide the leadership needed for your projects. We work closely with you to tailor a delivery system and staffing strategy that fits your requirements in the most cost-effective manner, allowing you to focus on your core business while we manage your project to a successful conclusion.


We develop a scope of work to meet your project’s specific needs and challenges, from preconstruction through construction closeout and project start-up, then serve as your on-site expert to manage your project’s daily execution to ensure the delivery of a quality-focused project within established time and budget constraints.
